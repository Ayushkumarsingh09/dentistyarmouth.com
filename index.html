Meet Dr. Jonathan Nutt

The Dentist Who
Puts People First

Known affectionately as "Dr. J," Jonathan Nutt founded Integrity Dental in January 2021 with one purpose: you.

Growing up in New Hampshire, Dr. J experienced dentistry the way it should be — personal, unhurried, and genuinely caring. When he earned his D.M.D. from the University of New England in 2018, he refused to accept that patient-centered care was a thing of the past.

He takes time to know each patient, listens to understand their goals, and develops customized plans that honor their needs and financial comfort. Whether catching up during checkups or perfecting a restoration, Dr. J is careful, gentle, and compassionate.

"I wanted to give my patients the same exceptional experience I had growing up — even if that meant going against the industry's new status quo."

— Dr. Jonathan Nutt, D.M.D.

Your First Visit

A Journey Designed
Around You

From the moment you arrive, every step is thoughtfully designed to help you feel comfortable, informed, and confident.

Step 01

Get To Know You

We start by listening. Your concerns, your goals, your story — we want to understand what matters most to you before we ever look inside your mouth.

Step 02

Comprehensive Evaluation

A thorough assessment including digital x-rays, oral cancer screening, muscle and joint evaluation, and a complete picture of your oral health.

Step 03

Personalized Treatment Plan

Together, we develop a customized plan tailored to your needs, goals, timeline, and budget — with complete transparency about every option.

Step 04

Long-Term Smile Success

We partner with you for life — celebrating your progress, adjusting your plan as needed, and ensuring your smile stays healthy for years to come.
